A BIT ABOUT YOU

I LOVE A GOOD STORY

I'm a creative and alternative wedding photographer, based in Sheffield, South Yorkshire and I travel the country taking photos to tell couples’ stories. From Manchester, the Lake District, to London and Edinburgh. My couples describe me as ‘different to the others’, I describe me as friendly, Yorkshire-made, sarcastic, and caffeine-fuelled.

YOU: THE UNCONVENTIONAL TYPE

If you’re here, you’re looking for award-winning natural photography of your wedding. A candid, photojournalistic-style approach that gives you an album of genuine, relaxed photographs of your family and friends, alongside some fun, creative shots of you two having an amazing time together - in most cases laughing at how awkward you might look (it’s ok, you never look awkward).

I don’t pose my couples and I absolutely love working with couples who are a bit camera shy, but like to have a laugh - they make the most amazing photos.

If that all sounds like you - then we’re a match! Check out my wedding info + pricing or read a bit more!
