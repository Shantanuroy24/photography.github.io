WEDDING PRICING + PACKAGES

AKA - HOW MUCH?

SO, YOU’RE IN THE RIGHT PLACE

The fun couples who live to push the rules are the ones I love to photograph. We’ll do portraits, but they’ll be relaxed and fun, there’s no posing, unless we’re being daft, but you can expect to be taking photos in places you wouldn’t dream of (the venue bin store - if it’s photogenic, we’re there). Don’t worry though, we’re not gone for hours, you’ll be back your guests and cocktails in no time!

For the rest of your wedding I’ll be watching for those stolen glances, friendly hugs and cheeky kisses, and, of course, the funny moments that will have you laughing for years to come.

NATURAL, CREATIVE + AUTHENTIC ONLY

WHAT’S THE DEAL?

Zero obligation consultation - face-to-face if you’re in South Yorkshire or via FaceTime.

Prep to Party - no running after the first dance, it’s all about the party - the dancing, the shots, and all the action.

Hand-edited, high-resolution photos - there’s no offshore editing or filter-slapping, each photograph is edited by me and delivered in high resolution to your gallery.

Online gallery - your photos are delivered through a secure online gallery, no more waiting for the postman or missing USBs. You can share (as much or as little of) your gallery with friends and family, download your photos, order prints and choose your album photos right from your phone/computer.

Tips and tricks, right when you need them - you get the benefit of all the awesome weddings I’ve captured over the years, from timeline tips, to how to get the best out of your portraits, to being the guy you can moan about ‘that aunt’ to. I’ve got you covered.

Previews within a week- a selection of the events of the day to satisfy the ‘gram and get your photography taste buds tingling, delivered the week after your wedding (usually within 3 days)
